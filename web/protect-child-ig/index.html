PROTECT-CHILD Transplant Data Implementation Guide (DRAFT)

Status: This Implementation Guide is a draft and may change based on ongoing work in PROTECT-CHILD.

This IG describes a FHIR-based representation of pediatric transplant data for the PROTECT-CHILD project.

At the centre is the TransplantCase logical model, which links:

  • Recipient & donor
    • Patient
    • Donor
  • Transplant & visits
    • Transplant (index transplant encounter)
    • Visit (pre- and post-transplant follow-up)
  • Concomitant disease & immunosuppression
    • ConcomitantDisease, ConcomitantEpisode, ConcomitantMedication
    • PreMedication, ImmunosuppressiveInductionPatient, ImmunosuppressiveMaintenancePatient, Immunosuppressant
  • Clinical data
    • ClinicalVariable
    • LabResultObservation, LabReport
    • Microbiology
    • PatientInstrumentalInvestigation
  • Biospecimens & omics
    • BioSample
    • GenomicTest, EpigenomeStudy, MethylomicStudy
    • VariantOccurrence, VariantAnnotation
    • ReferenceGenome
  • Events & outcomes
    • PostEvent
    • Outcome
